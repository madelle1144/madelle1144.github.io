About Our School

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers underprivileged children in Kenya, fostering academic excellence, moral values, and life skills that enable them to become productive members of society.

Our Vision

A world where every child, regardless of their background, has access to quality education and opportunities to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

Founded in 2010, Claires Organisation Kenya has grown from a small community initiative to a reputable educational institution serving over 500 students annually. Our holistic approach to education addresses not just academic needs but also the physical, emotional, and social well-being of our students.
